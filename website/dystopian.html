Dystopian

This genre includes books set in a fictional world usually in the future to explore social and political issues. Common themes in these types of books inlcude rebellion, oppression, revolutions, wars and disasters. If you enjoy books with a lot of action and thrills, this is a great genre to get into.

Legend: a triology followed by Prodigy and Champion. This story is about a boy name June who was lucky enough to be born into a wealthy family. Having no parents she is raised by her older brother. Since she was a child she has been training to get into the highest military circle in the Republic. On the opposite side of the city, Day comes from a poor area and has recently become one of the Republic's most wanted criminals. Soon their paths cross and in an unlikely twist of fate become friends. On run from the Republic, they aren't safe anywhere. How will they survive? What will happen to June and Day? How the stories end, well you can only find the answers on the book.

Uglies is an older series that came out in 2005, but is still a great read. In a future world where at 16 years old you are required by law to undergo cosmetic surgery to make yourself 'pretty' life seems simple. When main character Tally Youngblood meets a girl named Shay who wants to stay 'ugly', she discovers the truth about her society and how everything may not be as pretty as people want it to seem. With 4 books in this series, you can discover what happens to Tally and what secrets she uncoveres.
