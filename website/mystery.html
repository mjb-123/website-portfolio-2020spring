Mystery

This genre includes books that are usually about murder or crimes that need to be solved. With so many possible suspects or ones you never considered suspects, mystery novels are known for their huge plot twists. If you enjoy books that have a completely unexpected ending, this is a great genre to get into.

The Knife of Never Letting Go. This story follows a boy named Todd Hewitt. He is from a small settlement area called 'New World', a place newly discovered by humans. In this place all boys at the age of 13 become men. Learning about his civilizations brutal past, the native alien creatures that inhabit the land are blamed for the spread of a deadly virus that led to so many peoples deaths; particularly the females. One day on a walk with his dog, he discovers a 'Noiseless' place and his adoptive parents send him from the home he grew up in. When he discovers a girl on his escape, his entire world gets turned upside down. What other secrets are buried? If you want answers this is a great read.

John Green with another fantastic book; Paper Towns. Quentin is shy, very quiet, the person you forget is in the room with you. When the most popular and well-known girl in school Margo seeks him out for a prank, he finally feels noticed. The next day after Quentin has tasted what its like to live on the edge, he discovers that Margo has dissapeared. Finding her trail of clues she left behind, he takes it upon himself to find her and bring her back home. Although I put this in the mystery category, there is definitely a hint of romance in the story. Find out where Margo went and read this for yourself.
