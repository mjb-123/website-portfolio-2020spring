Romance

This genre includes books in which 2 or more characters relationship is the main focuse while other things like mystery or adventure is secondary. You are invested in their status and if they will work out or not. Everything else that happens to them is either preventing them from being together or helping them develop more. If you enjoy books with a love story, this is a great genre to get into.

The Sun Is Also a Star. In this fast paced book that takes place in a 24 hour period Daniel Bae and Natasha Kingsley meet in an unlikely circumstance. While she's on her way to try and stop her family from getting deported, he saves her life on his way to an interview. Being the romantic that he is, he tells her he can make her fall in love with him. Is it possible to fall for someone so quickly? Will Natasha help her family stay in America? The only way to find out is to read the book for yourself. This is one of my favorite books right now, it was a quick read and gave me everything I wanted.
